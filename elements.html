Introduction

I'm Sajith PE, a self-taught full-stack developer driven by a passion for solving digital challenges with modern technologies. I specialize in using a wide range of tools and frameworks like Node.js, React.js, Django, Express.js, TypeScript, Next.js, Redux, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Socket.io, and WebRTC. My journey into coding began with a life-changing moment—watching the 100k coding challenge video, which pulled me into the world of development like a magnet. I took my first steps with Brototype, and from there, I never looked back. Over time, I’ve developed strong architectural skills, including Microservices, Clean Architecture, and MVC Architecture. I’ve worked with powerful tools like Kafka, Redis, RabbitMQ, Celery, Docker, Kubernetes, AWS, and Databricks. I’m also experienced in database design and integrations with services such as Razorpay, Stripe, PayPal, Chargebee, S3, Cloudinary, Twilio, SendGrid, Firebase, Node Mailer, Google Dialogflow, Tawk, Zego Cloud, OAuth 2.0, JWT, Postman, and JIRA. I’m committed to writing clean, scalable, and maintainable code. I enjoy working in fast-paced environments, building projects from the ground up with a strong focus on CI/CD pipelines and cloud performance optimization. Beyond coding, I’m a passionate learner who’s always growing. I love contributing to open-source projects, joining coding competitions, and engaging with the developer community. Let’s connect and create something amazing together!
